Athenaeum

BMSCE's Official Online Virtual Library









The Aethenaeum initiative was started keeping the comfort of our students in mind. Going to a physical library might not be the most convinient option for some people, especially during the COVID pandemic. So,we thought why not bring the library to them? Our website consists of a wide variety of source materials that students can pick and choose from. Ranging from syllabus textbooks to extra e-resources, we're trying to extend the best resources to our students from the convinience of their homes or even their their hostel dorms.

As a part of our mission to increase the accessibility of materials to students we've also integrated a BOOK ECHANGE initiative where students can get connected with others to exchange course books, seek advice and get a chance to use the websites facilities to the fullest. 		ABOUT US
